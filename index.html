Field Engineer Instructions for Filling Out Module 2 (Pump Performance Test)
1. Selecting Facility & Pump Group

Select either **QPL Facility** or **SBPL Facility** at the top. For grouped pumps like **Feedwater Pumps** or **Fire Pumps**, tap the family button to reveal sub-pumps (Jockey, Motor-Driven, Diesel, Booster) and test each unit individually.

2. Unit Selectors & Meter Snapshots

Use the Global Unit Toggle (Metric vs English) or override individual fields (PSI, Bar, GPM, m³/hr, meters, feet). On phone/tablet devices, tap the camera icon (📷) to snap photos of physical gauges. Photos are automatically compressed and saved with XLSX exports.

3. Diagnostics & Multi-Scope XLSX Export

Review resolved Total Dynamic Head (TDH), Affinity Law corrections for shaft speed (RPM), motor electrical power factors, and NPSHa cavitation safety limits. Click **Export to XLSX** to export single pumps, entire pump families, or whole facility databases.
